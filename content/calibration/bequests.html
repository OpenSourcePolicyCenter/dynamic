Bequest Process Calibration¶

[TODO: This chapter needs to be finished.]

This chapter describes how we calibrate the distribution of total bequests \(BQ_t\) to each living household of age \(s\) and lifetime income group \(j\). The matrix that governs this distribution \(\zeta_{j,s}\) is seen in the household budget constraint EqHHBC.

A large number of papers study the effects of different bequest motives and specifications on the distribution of wealth, though there is no consensus regarding the true bequest transmission process. See [DeNardiY14], [DeNardi04], [Nis02], [Lai01], [GKSW00], [GS94a], [Hur89], [VW88], [KS81], and [Wol15].
